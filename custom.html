Here's the Plan

Once you submit your customization options, we’ll add it to our review queue. We’ll review the order to get a feel for what you’re looking for, and if we are ready to start building, we’ll send you an estimate detailing the cost. If it all makes sense to you, then we'll ask for payment.

Right now, we're only using PayPal (see the Questions? page). All fully paid designs will be moved to the build queue in the order of recieved payments. Both queues are worked from top down.
